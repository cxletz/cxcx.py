A video store needs a way to determine the amount to charge customers for DVD rentals.
The amount depends on the time of day the customer returns the DVD. There are also special rates on Thursday and Sundays.
The fee structure is as follows:

The default cost (cost_per_day) is $1.59 per night.
If the customer returns the DVD after 8PM, the store charges them for an extra night. This is stored in the ontime variable.
There is a 30% discount for DVDs rented on Sundays (Sunday), for the full length of the rental. The rental day is stored in the weekday variable.
There is a 50% discount for DVDs rented on Thursdays (Thursday), for the full length of the rental. The length of the rental is stored in the days_rented variable.

Complete the code to meet the requirements by selecting the correct code segment from each drop-down list. 
                
#DVD Rental Calculator
ontime = input("Was the video returned before 8 PM? Y or N").lower()
days_rented = int(input("How may days was the video rented"))
weekday = input("What day was the video rented?").capitalize()
cost_per_day = 1.59
if ontime 
    days_rented += 1
if weekday 
    total = (days_rented * cost_per_day) * 0.7
elif weekday 
    total = (days_rented * cost_per_day) * 0.5
else:
    total = days_rented * cost_per_day
print("Cost of the DVD is : $", total)
